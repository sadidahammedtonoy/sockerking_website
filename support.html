Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn the $10 credit?

expand_more
Simply go to your profile, tap on "Refer a Friend", and share your unique link. When they sign up using Google or Apple social login, you'll receive a $10 coupon automatically.

How do I cast videos to my TV?

expand_more
Open any training video, and you'll see the casting icon in the top right corner. Make sure your TV and phone are on the same Wi-Fi network.

Are the challenges live or recorded?

expand_more
Most challenges are curated video sessions that you can follow at your own pace. Some exclusive seasonal events may feature live components.

How do I change the app language?

expand_more
Go to Settings > Language. We currently support English and Polish, with more languages coming soon.
