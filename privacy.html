Privacy Policy

At Play Socker King, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy outlines how we handle the information collected from our users, particularly focused on our young athletes and their guardians.

What Information We Collect

  • Parent/Guardian Email: For account verification and security.
  • Child Details: Basic profile information to personalize training sessions.
  • App Usage: Information on how you interact with our training videos and challenges.
  • Device Info: Technical data for compatibility and performance optimization.

How We Use Information

  • To provide a personalized training experience.
  • To track and visualize your soccer skills progress.
  • To improve our overall app performance and training content.
  • To securely manage your subscriptions and rewards.

What We Do Not Do

  • No Selling of Data: We never sell your personal information to third parties.
  • No In-App Ads: Our training environment is 100% ad-free for focused learning.
  • No External Links: We maintain a safe environment by excluding external third-party links.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure your data remains protected at all times, preventing unauthorized access and ensuring data integrity.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can request access, correction, or deletion of your information at any time through our support team.
